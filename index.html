Regaining Lost Visitors Made Simple.

Do you like browsing many tabs at once? Do you usually forget about some of them? Turns out you are not the only one. Most of your users don't click the close button, they move to another tab; few minutes away, you are off their mind list.

With our tool, you can regain their attention and chance to convert them.

How It Works?
1. Our technology tracks when people are not viewing your page.
2. If people exceed the threshold of inactive time, we show a small notification with sound.
3. Just like Facebook, users immediately notice and come back to check what's new.
4. Show your offer or tell them what they were doing.
